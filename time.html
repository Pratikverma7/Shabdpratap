Timimgs :

The sole objective of Shabd Pratap Satsang is focussed on the sipiritual upliftment of the soul to reach at the sacred feet of the Supreme Lord. All the activities of Satsang are towards achieving this ultimate goal.
Religious Programmes as under are being rigorously , regularly and punctually observed as prescribed by Sant Satguru Huzur Data Dayal for over seven decades.





Timimgs :


S.no Time Location Event
1 4 a.m to 5.30 a.m Ashram Meditation of Surat Shabd Yog
2 6 a.m to 7.30 a.m Ashram Dhun
3 2 p.m to 4 p.m Samadh Satsang (Divine Services ) and meditation for and by ladies.
4 4 p.m to 5 p.m Samadh Dhun ( Recital of Holy Name & Religious devotional songs)
5 7 p.m to 8.30 p.m Ashram Satsang (Divine Services ) of both ladies & gents.
6 9 p.m to 9.30 p.m Samadh Prayer

NOTE : Besides above scheduled programmes general satsang is also held on sundays for 90 minutes either in morning or afternoon.
